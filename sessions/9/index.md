## Browsing session

We are going to browse some websites to expand our frame of reference.

Some questions to consider:

- What do you see?
- What do you like?
- What do you dislike?
- How is the site interactive?
- What is happening typographically?
- How is color being used?
- What is the grid?
- How does the site change with screen size?

Let's have a look and talk about what we're seeing.
